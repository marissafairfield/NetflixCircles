Introducing: Netflix Circles

Over the last 15 years, Netflix has become a streaming staple in most peoples’ homes. Well, please welcome to the Netflix family our new feature - Netflix Circles!

Add your friends!

You can now be social with your Netflix preferences! Share or receive a unique friend code to start building your Circle.

See what they’re watching!

With My Circle, users can access friends’ profiles showing their watch history, their personal recommendations for you, and recent shows or movies they Like and Love!

Recommendations made easy

Simply click the My Circle button featured on your movie or show selection to recommend to your friends!
